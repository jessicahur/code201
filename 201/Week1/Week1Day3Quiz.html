Interested in Learning?

CodeFellows now offers a three course solution that is designed to train those with little or no exposure to coding or JavaScript. The three courses include:

  • Code 201 (4 weeks)
  • Code 301 (4 weeks)
  • Code 401 (10 weeks)

What do you need to suceed?

Although no previous code writing experience is necessary, there are some thiings you will have to do to suceed in this course and to prepare yourself for a job as a software developer:

  • Complete the prework before class begins
  • Clear your life of distractions
  • Be prepared to devote 10-12 hours a day, 5-6 days a week

Background

Code fellows was established in 2013 in Seattle, Washington to help close a gap in the local market for software developers. The founders of Code Fellows worked with local companies to determine what skills where in highest demand. They developed an immersive course using a non-traditional approach to learning.

Options

There are options for those who want to learn full time during the day and for those that can only attend night courses. There are also scholarships available. Whatever your needs and schedules, they are working to meet your needs. If you have been considering learning to code, but a traditional classroom schedule and methods are not a good fit for you, look into Code Fellows
